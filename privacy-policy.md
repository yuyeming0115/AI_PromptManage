# Privacy Policy for AI Prompt Manager

**Last updated: March 19, 2026**

## Overview

AI Prompt Manager is a browser extension that helps you save and quickly access your AI prompts. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect

### Local Data
- Your prompts and categories are stored locally in your browser using `chrome.storage.local`.
- Your panel position and size preferences are stored locally.

### Cloud Sync (Optional)
If you choose to create an account and enable cloud sync:
- We store your **email address** for authentication purposes.
- We store your **prompts and categories** in a cloud database to enable cross-device sync.
- Cloud data is stored securely on [Supabase](https://supabase.com) with Row Level Security — only you can access your own data.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- We do not collect browsing history.
- We do not collect any data from the pages you visit.
- We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
- We do not use your prompts for any AI training or analytics.

## Data Storage & Security

- Local data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly enable cloud sync.
- Cloud data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and protected by Row Level Security policies.
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

## Third-Party Services

When cloud sync is enabled, data is stored using [Supabase](https://supabase.com). Please refer to [Supabase's Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy) for details on how they handle data.

## Your Rights

- **Access**: You can export all your data at any time using the built-in JSON export feature.
- **Deletion**: You can delete individual prompts or all data locally at any time. To delete cloud data, sign out and contact us.
- **Portability**: Your data can be exported as JSON and imported into any compatible tool.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue at our [GitHub repository](https://github.com).
